F.C. Internazionale Milano (Inter Milan)

Founded: March 9, 1908

Stadium: San Siro (Stadio Giuseppe Meazza)

Capacity: ~75,923 seats Shared with A.C. Milan Colors: Blue and Black

Nickname: I Nerazzurri (The Black and Blues)

Achievements:

Serie A Titles: 20 Coppa Italia Titles: 8 UEFA Champions League Titles: 3 (1963–64, 1964–65, 2009–10) FIFA Club World Cup Titles: 1 (2010)
